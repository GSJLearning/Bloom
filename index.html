About Us


We, of the GSJ Learning group, are a group of grade 12 software development student from iACADEMY. Our PBL for the semester is to create a program that would be in relation to sustainability. In regards to this, the GSJ Learning group has decided to create a website. Our website's goal is to be able to spread information in regards to sustaining the environment. It would be similar to a forum in which users would be able to interact with each other and share their knowledge regarding sustainable practices. Users would be able to post questions, and we, as the moderators, would check the posts and decide whether or not they would be appropriate enough to be posted. After the questions are posted, other users would be able to provide solutions based on their knowledge. Through this, we would be able to create a community where people would provide help for each other.
